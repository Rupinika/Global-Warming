The Impact

The impact of Global Warming is huge. It can effect our natural disasters and our coral reefs. A study that was conducted showed that by 2040 our natural disasters will become even more extreme and probaly move places such as going from a cold place to a usally much warmer one. Also our reefs will not be able to adapt to the sudden change and die out causeing a disturbance in the food chain.
